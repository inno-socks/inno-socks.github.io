Learn more about the Scotia Momentum Visa Infinite

The Scotia Momentum Visa Infinite card is our pick for best overall cash back
card due to its phenomenal cash back value. Using this card, you'll earn a
whopping 4% cash back on grocery purchases, recurring payments, and
subscriptions. Not only that, but you'll also get an impressive 2% on gas and daily
transit, with a 1% earn rate on everything else.

And if that isn't enough for you, new cardholders will be able to earn 10%
cash back on all purchases for the first 3 months (up to $2000 in total purchases). Plus,
no annual fee in the first year (including supplementary cards). Offer expires
August 31, 2022. That's an excellent incentive for anyone thinking of signing up.

Why we like it:

The card's generous welcome offer and standard earn rates are impressive
enough, but its comprehensive insurance coverage is what really sets it apart.
With the Scotia Momentum Visa Infinite, you'll receive new mobile device
insurance, purchase security and extended warranty protection, plus a thorough
travel insurance package including trip cancellation/interruption, travel
emergency medical, delayed/lost baggage, and much more.

Additional perks:

For those using the card to travel, you'll also enjoy complimentary Visa Infinite*
concierge service, hotel room upgrades (when available), and up to 25% off base
rates at participating AVIS locations and at participating Budget locations in
Canada and the U.S.
